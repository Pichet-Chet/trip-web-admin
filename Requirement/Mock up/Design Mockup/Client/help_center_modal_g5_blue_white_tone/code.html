Frequently Asked Questions

What to do if I'm lost? expand_more
Stay where you are and send your live location via the 'Chat' button above. Kenji will reach out immediately. Look for a landmark and mention it.
Where is the meeting point? expand_more
We meet at the Hachiko Statue, Shibuya Station Exit 8. Look for the 'Amazing Tour' gold-and-indigo flag.
Can I change my itinerary? expand_more
Yes, simple adjustments are possible. Please message Kenji at least 2 hours before the activity starts.
